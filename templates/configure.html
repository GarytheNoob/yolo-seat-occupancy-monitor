Instructions

  1. Capture Reference Frame: Click the "Capture Frame" button to get a snapshot from the camera

  3. Label Seats: Enter a descriptive label for each seat (e.g., "Seat 1", "Front Left")
  4. Adjust if Needed: Delete and redraw seats as needed
  5. Save: Click "Save Configuration" to apply your changes
